RESELLERS FAQ
LAST UPDATED: 2023-02-08

Do you work with resellers or distributors?
Yes, we do.
How to become a reseller/distributor of your products?
Just contact us and we’ll discuss all the details.
What discount do you offer for resellers?
By default it is 20% from the retail price. The discount rate may be changed later depending on “history” of our collaboration and the number of customers you bring to us.
How does the order process work? Can we separate the invoice and license details?
  • We give you a special coupon code for discount.
  • You place an order as regular customer (using the link on the product’s web-page) and apply that coupon code.
  • You sign in to our client area with the e-mail you used for purchasing.
  • You open the license details on the account page, and add your customer as license user.
  • Now your customer will see the license on his/her account page and will be able to download full version of software.
How can I get a quote?
At the end of the order page you will find "Get Quote" button. Fill out the form and press this button to get a quote.
